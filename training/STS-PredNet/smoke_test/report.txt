6. Errors, Failures, and Inconveniences Encountered

   [E-1] CUDA driver too old (SYSTEM LIMITATION)
         Symptom: "CUDA initialization: The NVIDIA driver on your system is
         too old (found version 12060)."
         Root cause: NVIDIA driver 12060 < 12070 required by PyTorch 2.12.1.
         Impact: All training runs on CPU (~0.7s/batch vs ~0.05s on GPU).
         Workaround: Set device: cpu in config and export CUDA_VISIBLE_DEVICES="".
         No script fix possible.

   [E-2] CPU training very slow with full 250-bin maps (PERFORMANCE LIMITATION)
         Symptom: 1 epoch of 1576 samples × batch=8 took ~140s on CPU.
         Root cause: 3×250 map × hidden_dim=16 × 2 PredRNN branches = heavy CPU compute.
         Impact: Full paper config (4 layers, 128 hidden, 500 epochs) would take
         weeks on CPU. A GPU is required for production training.
         Workaround: Reduced to 1 layer, 16 hidden, 2 epochs for smoke test.

   [E-3] GPU memory estimate (INFORMATIONAL)
         With full paper config (4 layers × 128 hidden × 3 branches), estimated
         batch=32 needs ~8-12 GB GPU memory. 3-node × 250-bin maps are 4% of
         the paper's 100×60 map area, so smaller than paper but deeper layers
         compensate.

   [E-4] Backward pass triggers CUDA init hang on old driver (SYSTEM LIMITATION)
         Symptom: loss.backward() hangs indefinitely when CUDA driver < 12070.
         Workaround: CUDA_VISIBLE_DEVICES="" prevents CUDA access entirely.

7. Issues and Notes
   - Positive R² (0.46) indicates the model captures meaningful signal even with
     46K parameters and 2 epochs — a good sign for the full model.
   - LW1 has higher RMSE (0.357) than CC1 (0.271) and CC2 (0.231), suggesting
     uneven spatial predictability across the 3 nodes.
   - The smoke test uses period_interval=10 (not 1440) to generate enough samples
     from 2000 rows. Production config uses period_interval=1440 with 6839 rows.
   - Trend branch remains disabled (needs 40320 min of history; AERPAW has 6839).
   - All spectrogram outputs and CSV exports verified correct shapes and ranges.
